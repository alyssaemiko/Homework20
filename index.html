my bloody valentine

band biography

  • Formed in Dublin in 1983
  • Signed to Creation Records in 1988
  • Credited with pioneering and defining the genre of music “shoegaze”

band members

  • Kevin Shields, guitar and vocals.
  • Bilinda Butcher, guitar and vocals.
  • Debbie Googe, bass.
  • Colm Ó Cíosóig, drums.

band chronology

  • 1991 - Loveless is released to high praise. Since it’s release, it has been in scores of “Best Album” lists, and has been called a “magnum opus.”
  • 1992 - Creating a follow up to the highly acclaimed Loveless, was disastrous. The band was said to have recorded over 60 hours of music before breaking up in 1995 and reuniting in 2008.
  • 1990’s - While the band was on extended hiatus, Kevin Shields was often compared to eccentric, reclusive musicians, such as Syd Barrett of Pink Floyd or Brian Wilson of the Beach Boys.
  • 2000's - My Bloody Valentine reunite at Coachella in 2008 and release the much anticipated m  b  v in 2013.

albums

  • Isn't Anything, 1988.
  • Loveless, 1991.
  • m  b  v, 2013.
